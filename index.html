About Me

Full Stack Developer based out of NC

Hi, I'm Grace, a proud Filipino and an experienced and enthusiastic former Preschool teacher turned Web Developer. I have backgrounds both in Psychology and Early Childhood Education, which gave me superpowers to enhance teaching and learning strategies by using my coding abilities. During my academic career in Thinkful's Remote Engineering program, I have led and contributed to several different team projects that helped me be a versatile, well-rounded team member; collaborating with like-minded, passionate developers is something I am very excited about.

This portfolio represents a few of the responsive and full-stack web applications I developed independently and in team projects.

When I'm not coding, I'm often curled up in a cozy chair, binge-watching detective-mystery films, walking in the park, or cooking heartwarming foods for my family. If you want to know more about my experience, I would love to chat!

My Projects

Gotta Lotto

GottaLotto is a team project built to show our knowledge of Data Structure and Algorithms by creating a guessing game app. GottaLotto is for lotto enthusiasts to have plain old fun in guessing the winning numbers for each Saturday's PowerBall's drawing. It allows users to submit their guesses for the upcoming drawing and a text area where users can include their lottery experience message. Submitted guesses are compared and scored based on how close they get from the Powerball winning numbers.

Start Small

As a former teacher at a school that serves low-income communities, I experienced a lack of classroom supplies to help my students keep on learning. Start small is an independent project built to help fund classroom projects posted by students or teachers from across the country. The app shows classroom project listings that users can review based on 1 to 5 stars. It also provides an external link to the specific projects donating site.

Client Server Live

Scrabble Solver

Scrabble solver is a team project that is built and inspired by the popular word game Scrabble. The application produces valid words based on the provided scrabble letters or tiles. It shows dictionary definitions when clicking on valid word results and allows scrabble enthusiasts to sort word results by score or length.

COVID-19 Quizapp

It is an independent project to showcase my knowledge of JQuery and inspired by the recent outbreak of the respiratory illness called COVID-19. The short quiz is to test a user's basic knowledge about the mentioned infectious disease. Users are notified if they have answered the word correctly or incorrectly and their scores are updated accordingly.
